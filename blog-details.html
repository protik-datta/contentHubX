Cooking pasta might seem simple, but mastering it takes a bit of practice. From choosing the right pasta to cooking it al dente, small details make a big difference.

Always salt your water generously and avoid adding oil. Stir the pasta occasionally to prevent sticking and reserve some pasta water for your sauce.

Finally, combine the pasta with sauce while it is still hot and let it finish cooking together for the best flavor.
